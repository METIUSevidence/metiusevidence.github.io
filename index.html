About METIUS

METIUS is a £11.5 million UKRI-funded infrastructure initiative designed to revolutionise how scientific evidence is synthesised and delivered to policymakers in the UK and globally.

    • Accelerate evidence synthesis
    • Produce “living” evidence updates
    • Translate complex research into actionable insights

Consortium

Led by Queen’s University Belfast in collaboration with global partners.

  • Campbell Collaboration
  • University College London
  • Pan-African Collective for Evidence
  • King’s College London
  • Newcastle University
  • University of Exeter
